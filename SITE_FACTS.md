## Services Offered

### Category 1: Breast Reconstruction
Comprehensive breast reconstruction for breast cancer patients, emphasizing compassionate, patient-centered care with expertise in both traditional and advanced microsurgical techniques.
- URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction`

**Procedures:**

1. **Natural Tissue Flap Reconstruction** — Uses patient's own tissue (abdomen, back, thighs) to rebuild the breast. DIEP Flap (preferred), TRAM Flap, LAT Flap, PAP Flap. DIEP procedure is 6-8 hours, recovery 4-6 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/natural-tissue-flap`

2. **Implant-Based Reconstruction** — Direct-to-implant or two-stage (tissue expander then permanent implant). Saline or silicone options. 1-2 hours per breast, recovery 2-3 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/implant-based`

3. **Oncoplastic Breast Reduction** — Combines lumpectomy with breast reshaping. Wise Pattern, Vertical Pattern, or Circumareolar techniques. Often covered by insurance.
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/oncoplastic`

4. **Resensation (Nerve Restoration)** — Nerve grafting technology to restore sensation to the nipple and breast area after mastectomy. Performed during reconstruction with no additional incisions. Sensation may return over months to two years.
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/resensation`

5. **Revision Breast Reconstruction** — Corrects issues from prior reconstruction: symmetry, implant complications, tissue flap changes, pain, aging/weight changes. Techniques include implant exchange, capsular contracture correction, fat grafting, scar revision.
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/revision`

6. **Specialized Procedures** — Aesthetic flat closure, nipple-areolar reconstruction (surgical + 3D tattooing), male breast reconstruction, lymphedema surgery (LVB, VLNT, liposuction, hybrid approaches).
   - URL: `/services/breast-reconstruction/specialized`

### Category 2: Aesthetic Breast Surgery
Breast enhancement procedures tailored to individual aesthetic goals.
- URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast`

**Procedures:**

1. **Breast Augmentation** — Saline or silicone implants, submuscular or subglandular placement. Partners with Mentor, Allergan, Sientra, and Motiva. Recovery 1-2 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast/augmentation`

2. **Breast Lift (Mastopexy)** — Removes excess skin, tightens tissue, repositions nipples. 2-3 hours, recovery 1-2 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast/lift`

3. **Breast Reduction** — Removes excess tissue, fat, and skin. Liposuction, vertical (lollipop), or anchor (inverted-T) techniques. 3-4 hours, may be covered by insurance.
   - URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast/reduction`

4. **Breast Implant Removal (Explant)** — Simple explantation, en bloc capsulectomy, or total capsulectomy. 1-2 hours.
   - URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast/implant-removal`

5. **Gynecomastia** — Male breast reduction via liposuction, excision, or combination. 1-3 hours, recovery 1-2 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/aesthetic-breast/gynecomastia`

### Category 3: Face
Facial rejuvenation and contouring procedures.
- URL: `/services/face`

**Procedures:**

1. **Facelift (Rhytidectomy)** — Traditional, mini, SMAS facelift. Lifts and repositions facial tissues, smooths folds, restores youthful contour.
   - URL: `/services/face/facelift`

2. **Neck Lift** — Removes excess fat, tightens sagging skin, eliminates vertical muscle bands. Often combined with facelift.
   - URL: `/services/face/neck-lift`

3. **Rhinoplasty** — Closed, open, septoplasty, or revision. 1.5-3 hours. Full results visible within one year.
   - URL: `/services/face/rhinoplasty`

4. **Blepharoplasty (Eyelid Surgery)** — Upper, lower, double eyelid, or combined. 1-3 hours. Can improve vision.
   - URL: `/services/face/blepharoplasty`

5. **Otoplasty (Ear Surgery)** — Reshapes prominent or protruding ears. Suturing or cartilage sculpting. Ages 5-7+ and adults.
   - URL: `/services/face/otoplasty`

6. **Chin Liposuction** — Permanent fat removal beneath chin for jawline definition. Small, discreet incisions.
   - URL: `/services/face/chin-liposuction`

### Category 4: Body
Body contouring and sculpting procedures.
- URL: `/services/body`

**Procedures:**

1. **Liposuction** — Traditional, tumescent, ultrasound-assisted (UAL), power-assisted (PAL). Permanent fat cell removal from abdomen, thighs, hips, arms, neck.
   - URL: `/services/body/liposuction`

2. **Lipo 360** — Circumferential liposuction of entire midsection (abdomen, flanks, lower back). Creates sculpted waistline.
   - URL: `/services/body/lipo-360`

3. **Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL)** — Liposuction + fat transfer to buttocks. Subtle, moderate, or pronounced options. Hip dip correction available.
   - URL: `/services/body/bbl`

4. **Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)** — Full, mini, or extended. Removes excess skin/fat, tightens muscles. Recovery 1-2 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/body/tummy-tuck`

5. **Mommy Makeover** — Combines tummy tuck, breast lift/augmentation, liposuction. 3-6 hours, recovery 1-2 weeks.
   - URL: `/services/body/mommy-makeover`

6. **Body Lift** — For 100+ pound weight loss patients. Includes panniculectomy, lower body lift, abdominoplasty, thigh lift, arm lift. Recovery 3-4 weeks initial, 6-8 weeks full.
   - URL: `/services/body/body-lift`

7. **Thigh Lift (Thighplasty)** — Medial, extended/vertical, or lateral. Removes excess skin, eliminates friction/chafing.
   - URL: `/services/body/thigh-lift`

8. **Arm Lift (Brachioplasty)** — Full brachioplasty, short scar, or arm liposuction. Removes excess skin and fat from upper arms.
   - URL: `/services/body/arm-lift`

## FAQ Content

**Q: What procedures do you offer at Specialized Plastic Surgery?**
We offer a wide range of cosmetic and reconstructive procedures, including breast augmentation, breast reconstruction, body contouring, facelifts, tummy tucks, liposuction, rhinoplasty, and more across our NJ and NY locations.

**Q: Is the consultation free?**
Consultation fees vary depending on the procedure. Please contact us to confirm details and schedule your appointment. Breast reconstruction and insurance-related consultations include free insurance verification.

**Q: How long is the recovery after surgery?**
Recovery times vary by procedure. Generally, patients can expect 1-2 weeks for initial healing, with full results visible in a few months. Our ERAS protocols help accelerate recovery.

**Q: Are the results permanent?**
While many procedures offer long-lasting results, maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help prolong your outcomes. Liposuction permanently removes fat cells, and breast implants typically last 10-20 years or longer.

**Q: Do you accept insurance for plastic surgery procedures?**
We are an out-of-network provider with most major carriers. Breast reconstruction is covered by law under WHCRA. Breast reduction and certain body contouring procedures may be covered when medically necessary. Our team handles the entire insurance process.

**Q: Are the procedures safe?**
All our procedures are performed by board-certified surgeons using the latest techniques and safety protocols. Our NY office features an AAAASF-accredited private surgical suite. We follow ERAS (Enhanced Recovery After Surgery) protocols for optimal safety and comfort.

**Q: How do I know which procedure is right for me?**
Our surgeons provide personalized consultations to assess your goals, anatomy, and options, ensuring the best approach for your needs. We take the time to listen to your concerns and develop customized treatment plans.

## ERAS (Enhanced Recovery After Surgery)

A patient-centered, evidence-based approach used at the practice. Three phases:

**Phase 1 - Before Surgery:** Nutritional screening, protein-rich diet, vitamin supplementation, medication review, discontinue interfering supplements/blood thinners.

**Phase 2 - During Surgery:** Opioid-sparing anesthesia with regional blocks, minimally invasive techniques, careful IV fluid management.

**Phase 3 - After Surgery:** Early mobility (within hours), multimodal pain management with fewer narcotics, comprehensive aftercare instructions.

**Benefits:** Faster recovery, reduced complications, better pain control, shorter hospital stays, improved patient satisfaction.
